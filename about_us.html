About UDV Technologies

About us

  • First opened in 2021 in Cyprus, UDV Technologies aimed to offer a wide range of IT and cybersecurity services to our clients in the region, and to establish ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking to safeguard their digital assets.
  • Building on this success, the Company has taken another challenge, expanding to the Middle East. In 2023, another UDV Technologies office started operation in Dubai, UAE, with the goal of offering our services to businesses that were looking for top-quality IT and cybersecurity solutions.
  • Establishing our presence in Dubai allowed the Company to tap into the rapidly growing market for IT and Cybersecurity services. With our proven track record of delivering high-quality solutions and services, we are confident that we can make a real difference to businesses.
  • Today, we are proud to have a strong presence in both Europe and the Middle East, working as an authorized partner of the world-recognized IT vendors such as One Identity, Check Point and Tenable. We aim to continue our business development and expand our operations in order to meet the ever-changing needs of our Customers. UDV Technologies remains committed to delivering the very best IT and cybersecurity solutions and services to businesses across the globe, and to helping them stay ahead of the curve in this fast-moving digital landscape.

Mission

We provide services of exceptional quality in the fields of cybersecurity and system integration to build trust relationships with our Customers. To reach this goal we unite sophisticated technologies with our own detailed approach and commitment to project and Customer success.
